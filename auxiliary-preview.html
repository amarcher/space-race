1000 Light-Years

Race to the stars

Setup

Shuffle the deck. Deal 6 cards to each pilot. Choose who goes first.

Your Turn

  1. Draw one card.
  2. Play one card or discard one.
  3. Pass to the next pilot.

Hazards

An opponent may play a hazard on you. You cannot advance until you play the matching remedy.

Safeties

Reveal a safety to become permanently immune to its matching hazard.
