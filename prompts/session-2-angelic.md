Blown-out angelic golden light streaming from behind — volumetric god-rays, halo effect, cinematic rim lighting. Golden-hour palette. MTG mythic-rare / Marvel Snap card-art quality. **Pick the variant with:**
- Clearest halo / god-ray effect behind the shuttle
- Red cross reads **immediately** — this is the brand mark of the card
- Golden hour feels warm, not cheesy (no orange crush)
- Shuttle design is plausible (not melting, not alien)
- Composition works cropped to a circular art-well
