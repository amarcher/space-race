Space Race — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Space Race is a single-player card game. It has no accounts, no sign-in, and it never asks for — or stores — your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information.

What the game stores on your device

Your gameplay preferences (selected game modes and the mute setting) are saved locally on your device so the game remembers them between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

The game uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics — things like how many games are played and on which platform (web or iOS). These statistics are not linked to your identity, are not used for advertising, and are not used to track you across other apps or websites. The web version at game.spaceexplorer.tech also uses Vercel Analytics for the same anonymous purpose.

No tracking, no ads, no third-party sharing

Space Race contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no trackers. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties beyond the anonymous analytics described above.

TV mode (optional)

The optional big-screen mode connects to a device on your own home network to mirror the game onto a TV. iOS will ask for Local Network permission before this happens. No data leaves your network.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages and collects the same minimal, anonymous data from every player.
